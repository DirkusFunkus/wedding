Our Story

Courtney and Jeremy first met through mutual friends at a New Years Eve party in 2012. It was not until months later on Courtney's 22nd birthday that their spark ignited on the dance floor of their favorite local pub Barley Island. At this time Courtney was living in her own apartment while Jeremy had been staying with a friend after returning from a year of traveling. Things were off to such a good start they went on their first date the very next day at the Indianapolis Zoo. Things escalated quickly as they decided to move into Courtney's apartment together that next night and they have been living together and sharing their lives each day since for six years. In that six years they have moved across country and back sharing five different homes together and raising two precious fur babies, Willow and Ash. While living in Colorado Springs CO for two years they traveled the state going on wonderous day hikes and camping adventures. They even backpacked through the Pecos Wilderness in Santa Fe National Forest of New Mexico before returning home to Indiana. Courtney and Jeremy love to have fun together and experience as may adventures as they can. Most of all they love to keep each other happy and that is why they can not wait to share their love with you in a celebration of marriage.
